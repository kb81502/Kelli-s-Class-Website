Casting: With the casting as Maxwell Caulfield and Michelle Pfeiffer as the leads, the movie is given an air of seriousness. As a contrast the other characters bring comedic relief to the movie and bring the same energy of the original Grease.

Plot: Another reason that makes this movie good is the plot that is mixed with heartfelt scenes, funny scenes, and scenes that are meant to be serious but are so bad that they are funny.

Music: The singing from the main characters is very well done and a few of the songs are very catchy or heart-felt.

Some of the good songs are: "Cool Rider" and "(Love Will) Turn Back The Hand Of Time "
