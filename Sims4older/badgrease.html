Plot: Some of the scenes in Grease 2 are either poorly acted or are weird relating to the rest of the movie. The message that Michael needs to change for Stephanie is not all that great.

Songs: Some of the songs are needlessly vulgar or just have very odd lyrics. They work to move the plot along but can become laughable far too easily.

Some of the bad but laughable songs are: "Prowling" and "Reproduction"
